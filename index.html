About Me

I am currently a research fellow at Sickkids Hosptial under the supervison of Prof. Eleanor Pullenayegum. I received my Ph.D in Statistics at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at McGill University under the supervision of Prof. Masoud Asgharian and Prof. Abass Khalili. My research interests are Longitudinal Data Analysis, Biased Sampling, and Variable Selection Methods. You can find my CV here.
